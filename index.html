About ICSAN

The Institute of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators of Nigeria (ICSAN) is a leading recognized professional body in Nigeria dedicated to enhancing the status and practice of Corporate Governance and Public Administration.

ICSAN is the only professional body authorized in Nigeria to conduct the examinations leading to qualification of Chartered Secretaries and Administrators.
